# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors are expected to keep collaboration direct, respectful, and technically grounded.

## Expected behavior

- focus discussion on the code, behavior, and evidence
- provide actionable review feedback
- respect maintainers, contributors, and users
- document assumptions and risks clearly

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- bad-faith review behavior or intentional disruption
- publishing private or security-sensitive information without authorization

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block access for behavior that undermines the repository’s working standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
